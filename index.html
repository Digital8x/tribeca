About The Project

Tribeca The Everett

Tribeca The Everett is an ultra-luxury residential development located in the prestigious neighborhood of Lulla Nagar, Pune. Spread across approximately 3.6 acres, this premium project features 2 elegant towers rising up to 29 floors.

Designed for modern luxury living, the project combines sophisticated architecture, expansive homes, premium specifications, and a curated selection of amenities. Every detail is planned to deliver exclusivity, comfort, and a truly elevated lifestyle.
